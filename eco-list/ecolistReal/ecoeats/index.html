Steak

Recipe Description

Gordon Ramsay’s method for cooking a perfect, yet simple fillet that pairs well with many sides of your liking.


  1. Prepare 1 fillet of steak at room temperature.
  2. Heat frying pan to medium heat.
  3. Add 15 ml oil, whole garlic clove, and herb sprig.
  4. Season both sides of fillet with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook each side for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Sear the rounded edges and add a knob of butter.
  7. Allow the butter to foam and baste both sides of steak thoroughly.
  8. Move steak to a plate and allow it to rest for at least 5 minutes.
  9. Serve along with any sides of your liking.

Tomato Fettuccine

Recipe Description

Using a variety of tomatoes brings a depth of flavour and great colour to this dish.


  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Rinse tomatoes and pat dry.
  3. Leave cherry tomatoes whole and chop larger tomatoes into 1 to 1 1/2-inch chunks.
  4. Place tomatoes and garlic in a 9x9-inch baking dish and sprinkle with salt, pepper and chili flakes.
  5. Toss to coat. Pour in enough oil to reach halfway up the tomatoes, about 1 cup.
  6. Stir to ensure all the tomatoes are coated.
  7. Bake for 1 hour, stirring halfway through, or until tomatoes are very tender.
  8. Remove from oven. Stir in 1/2 cup torn basil leaves and let rest for 20 minutes.
  9. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook fettuccine according to package directions, about 8 minutes. Drain and return to pasta pot.
  10. Lift tomatoes from oil using a slotted spoon and add to pasta.
  11. Toss the pasta, adding tomato oil by the tablespoon until the pasta is coated and flavourful, up to 1/3 cup. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Spoon into pasta bowls. Sprinkle with Parmigiano-Reggiano if desired. Garnish with remaining basil.

Grilled Cheese Sandwiches

Recipe Description

This is a great low carb solution that still allows you to enjoy a comforting grilled cheese sandwich.


  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Place cauliflower into food processor and pulse until crumbs about half the size of a grain of rice.
  2. Place cauliflower into large microwave safe bowl and microwave for 2 minutes. Your cauliflower should be soft and tender.
  3. Stir cauliflower to mix up the bottom and top cauliflower. Place back into the microwave and cook for another 3 minutes. Remove and stir again so that all the cauliflower cooks evenly.
  4. Place back into microwave and cook for 5 minutes. At this point, you should see the cauliflower is starting to become more dry.
  5. Microwave for another 5 minutes. Cauliflower should still be slightly moist to the touch, but should look dry and clumped up.
  6. Allow cauliflower to cool for a few minutes. Then add in egg, parmesan and seasoning. Stir to combine until smooth paste divs.
  7. Divide dough into 4 equal parts. Place onto large baking sheet lined with parchment paper or silpat mat. Using your knuckles and fingers, shape into square bread slices about 1/2 inch thick.
  8. Bake cauliflower bread for about 15-18 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool a few minutes.
  10. Using a good spatula, carefully slide cauliflower bread off of parchment paper. Now you are ready to assemble your sandwiches.
  11. Place sandwiches into toaster oven and broil for several minutes (5-10) until cheese is completely melted and bread is toasty.

Recipe Description

Braised Paprika Chicken

Recipe Description

Sweet Hungarian paprika gives this creamy braised chicken the best flavor.


  1. Pat chicken pieces dry with paper towels and season with 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a large heavy casserole or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onions and sprinkle with sugar.
  3. Cook, stirring frequently, until the onions are very soft and light brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in bell peppers, tomato paste, paprika and crushed red pepper. Add the chicken and stir it gently into the onion mixture.
  5. Sprinkle with marjoram and add broth. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and simmer over medium-low heat until the chicken is very tender, about 50 minutes.
  6. Just before the chicken is done, whisk sour cream, flour and the remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt in a small bowl until smooth.
  7. When the chicken is done, remove it to a plate. Stir the sour cream mixture into the sauce; return to a simmer and cook, stirring, until the sauce coats the spoon.
  8. Reduce heat to low, return the chicken to the sauce and reheat, about 1 minute. Serve garnished with parsley, dill and/or chives, if desired.
