Our Apps

EcoList, EcoEats, and Donations are our three main features. A majority of food waste occurs when consumers purchase an excessive amount of unnecessary food so we designed our apps to reduce food waste through better planning and organization of buying groceries.

EcoList

EcoList is a shopping list which enables you to add items and quantities to your list and check them off as you shop. Like a traditional shopping list, you can keep track of all the items you're buying easily and efficiently.

EcoEats

EcoEats is a meal planner that features over forty recipes from 7 different diets. The ingredients on that page can be added to your shopping list with just a couple clicks. You are also able to make your own meal plans and save them on our site.

Donation

We also feature a donations page where you can find all the places in the Vancouver area where you can go to donate food.
